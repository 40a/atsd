# Requirements


| Deployment | Single-Node | Cluster |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Nodes | 1 | > 5 |
| Processor | 10+ GHz | 10+ GHz |
| RAM | 4 GB (min 1 GB) | 16 GB |

> Total Processor capacity computed as number of cores multiplied by clock speed, e.g. 4 x 2.5GHz = 10 GHz
>
